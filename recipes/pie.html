Pie

This is the best lemon pie ever! It is always a hit. It is a family recipe that even those who do not like lemon pie, love. A must try!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup lemon
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 cup sour cream
  1. Pour milk, sugar, butter, lemon n yolks, in a sauce pan to thick
  2. Fold in sour cream; pour into the baked pie crust. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  3. Spread meringue on top
  4. Broil the meringue
